Our Story

Founded in 2010, BiteTime started as a small family-owned food truck with a simple mission: to serve delicious, high-quality fast food that doesn't compromise on taste or ingredients.

Over the years, we've grown into a beloved local establishment, but we've never lost sight of our roots. Every dish we serve is prepared with the same care and attention to detail that we started with.

We believe that fast food should be both convenient and exceptional. That's why we source our ingredients locally whenever possible and prepare everything fresh daily.
